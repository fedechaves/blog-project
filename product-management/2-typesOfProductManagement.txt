three different types of product Manager roles: 
    1- Internal product managers
        stakeholders: is the objective..legal team, marketing, ceo etc. 
        tasks: internal tools, working for people within the company. 
    2-Consumer PMs
        stakeholders: average consumer.
        tasks: vision and creativity, metrics, talking testing users 
    3- B2B PMs
        stakeholders: clients are other companies.
        tasks: salespeople in own companie,
