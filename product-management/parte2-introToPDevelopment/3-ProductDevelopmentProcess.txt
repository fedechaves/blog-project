Product Development process

Conceive 
    collect user problems, brainstorm solutions, identify insight of employees

plan
    market research, business case, customer interview, how long it may take, timeline

Development
    timelines, features, user stories, specs, estimations on details, develop, validation on early products-

Iterate
    MVP finished, early feedback, testing, Alpha Beta, A/B test

Lauch
    sell team, marketing team, legal team, PR, position product for public lunch, test reaction

Steady State
    collecting metrics, buying behaviour, try to maximize return, 

Maintain or kill
    decide how frequenly people are purchasing, is it profitable? it may not have anything to do with profit or users it may change the vision 
    of the company, sunseting: kill the product slowly, tell the customers etc
