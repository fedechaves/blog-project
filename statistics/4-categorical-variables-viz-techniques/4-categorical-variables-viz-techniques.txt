Categorical viz most used representations:

1-frequency distribution tables
    two columns
        category and frequency - > example bmw  729 sold
                                
2-bar charts
    we can use barchart using frequency (quanty car solds) in y axes and the category in x axis
    
3-pie charts
    piechart using relative frequency (%)

4-pareto (80-20 rule) diagrams
    in the same diagram we can visualize pareto distribution with cumulative frequency (sum of relative frequencies)
